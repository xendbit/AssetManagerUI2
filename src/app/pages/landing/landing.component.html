Token Management Information

Nifty Row Token (NRT) will be managed by a Decentralized Autonomous Organization (DAO) that will operate through on-chain governance which will provide ongoing support for the creative community.

The NRT will enable:
  • Early access to presales
  • Authorized Collections
  • Voting on proposals
  • Customized rarity
  • Guaranteed royalties
  • NFT Lots
  • Discounted Fees
  • Exclusive Access to Mint
  • Funding of Creative Initiatives and Projects
