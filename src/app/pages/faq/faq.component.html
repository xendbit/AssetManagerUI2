What are NFTs?
NFTs (Non-fungible Token) are a unique digital asset that acts as a certificate of ownership for Metaverse(physical or virtual) assets like photos, drawings, arts, videos, Tweets, etc.. As an artist, by tokenizing your work you both ensure that it is unique and brand it as your work. The actual ownership is blockchain managed. Each NFT is unique and maintained in a digital ledger called a blockchain.

How is NFT different from Cryptocurrency?

Cryptocurrencies can be traded or exchanged for one another and are always equal in value. One Ethereum is equal to another ETH. NFTs are different. Each has a distinct signature that makes it impossible for it to be equal to another.

What does Nifty Row do?

Nifty Row empowers the art community to leverage blockchain, authenticity, and provenance of artwork while improving time to market.
